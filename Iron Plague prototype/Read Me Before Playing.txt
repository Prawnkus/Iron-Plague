Welcome to our prototype!

After completing the prototype, we hope you would like to give us somefeedback on

How to Play:
- Movement: WASD - keys
- Shoot: movement of mouse + Left-click to shoot
- Power Up ability: Q - key

The goal of this prototype is for you to kill all the enemies in the game.
